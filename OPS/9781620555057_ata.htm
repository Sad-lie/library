ABOUT THE AUTHOR

STEPHEN HARROD BUHNER is the senior researcher for the Foundation for Gaian Studies. He is the award-winning author of 20 books, including Healing Lyme, Healing Lyme Disease Coinfections, Herbal Antibiotics, The Secret Teachings of Plants, and Plant Intelligence and the Imaginal Realm. He has taught for more than 30 years throughout North America and Europe on herbal medicine, the sacredness of plants, and the intelligence of nature. He lives in Silver City, New Mexico.
