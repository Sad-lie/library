6. How to Read Rhythm

 

Reading the notes on a staff is more than merely reading their pitches. One of the hardest things for many people who are learning to read music is to make sense of the rhythmic variation on the page. Music is a temporal art, and as such there is a huge variety of rhythmic patterns that musicians employ. Common notation has a way of notating all of them, or virtually all of them, and it can seem confusing at first. Once you get a handle on the way rhythm is notated, however, and once you build it into your practice of reading the page, it will begin to flow naturally.
