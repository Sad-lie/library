1.8. Clef

 

The clef in which a piece is written determines the note values of the lines on the staff (and the respective spaces between those lines). The clef is the very first thing that is written on a staff – it is the furthest thing to the left of the page, and it is written on each line of the staff down the page. Clefs are the crucial element of the staff and they will be explained in-depth in a dedicated section.
