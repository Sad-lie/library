I will tell you for the record that there are structures underneath the surface of Mars.… I will also tell you that there are machines under the surface of Mars that you can look at. You can find out in detail, you can see what they are, where they are, who they are and a lot of detail about them … you can do that through remote viewing.

—Major General Albert Stubblebine, U.S. Army, commander of INSCOM, 1981–84
